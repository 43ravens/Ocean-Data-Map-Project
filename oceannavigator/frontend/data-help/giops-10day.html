Global Ice Ocean Prediction System (GIOPS)

How the GIOPS 10 day forecasts are derived.

  • For 2D, the first four time steps (003, 006, 009, 012) are taken from the 00 and 12 simulation runs and combined to create combined to create the 10 day forecast.
  • For 3D, the first five 24 hour time steps (024, 048, 072, 096, 120) from the 00 and 12 simutation runs and combined to create the 10 day forecast.

Global Ocean 1/4° Physics Analysis and Forecast Updated Daily

Variables and derived products from GIOPS:

  • derived deep sound channel (m)
  • derived deep sound channel bottom (m)
  • water temperature (°C)
  • water salinity (psu)
  • derived sonic layer depth (m)
  • derived speed of sound (m)
  • water east velocity (m/s)
  • water north velocity (m/s)
  • derived water velocity (m/s)

Geographical Coverage: global ocean

Spatial Resolution: 1/4 degree latitude and longitude

Vertical Coverage: 50 levels ranging from 1 m to 250 m thickness at depth

Temporal Resolution: Daily mean for all fields, and 3 hr average surface fields

Temporal Coverage: From 2014-03-12 to present

Data Source: Environment and Climate Change Canada
